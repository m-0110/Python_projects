India Deaths Analysis

Details regarding the deaths due to each factor in India from 1990 to 2017.

The top five major risk factors for deaths in India are Air pollution, High systolic blood pressure, Smoking, Unsafe water source, High fasting plasma glucose. The highest deaths in India are recorded due to are Air pollution, High systolic blood pressure, Smoking. The least deaths are recorded due to Discontinued breastfeeding.
