World Deaths Analysis

Details regarding the deaths due to each factor in World from 1990 to 2017.

The top five major risk factors for deaths in India are High systolic blood pressure, Smoking, High fasting plasma glucose, Air pollution, High body-mass index. The highest deaths in India are recorded due to are High systolic blood pressure, Smoking, High fasting plasma glucose. The least deaths are recorded due to Discontinued breastfeeding.
